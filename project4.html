Guidance Glasses

Description

Guidance Glasses was developed to aid visually impaired individuals in navigating urban environments safely. Our device integrates a camera, ultrasonic sensor, microphone, and haptic feedback to alert users about their surroundings.

As hardware lead, I designed and prototyped the smart glasses housing to integrate all electronics. Through five iterative frame designs, I evenly distributed the weight of the components to balance the left and right frames.

I actively coordinated and worked with a multidisciplinary team of five engineers, ensuring clear communication to accelerate development. By using SolidWorks, I was able to rapidly prototype the device for the team to test the device.

Skills

SolidWorks, 3D Printing, GD&T, Rapid Prototyping
